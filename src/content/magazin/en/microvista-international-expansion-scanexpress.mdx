## Microvista expands international presence with mobile CT system SCANEXPRESS

([Blankenburg (Harz)](https://www.pressebox.de/pressemitteilung/suche/schlagwoerter/Blankenburg%20%28Harz%29), 27.03.2025) Microvista, the German specialist for industrial computed tomography and non-destructive testing, announces the Europe-wide expansion of its innovative mobile CT system _**SCAN**EXPRESS_. The system is already being used successfully at a French automotive supplier.

The _SCANEXPRESS_ represents a significant step forward in industrial inspection technology by enabling high-precision CT analysis directly at the point of use – without the usual logistical challenges and transport risks.

"With the _SCANEXPRESS_ we bring the CT laboratory to the customer and not the other way around," explains Microvista's managing director, Prof. Dr. Lutz Hagner. "This flexibility is particularly valuable for companies where the transport of components to an external test laboratory would disrupt production processes or endanger sensitive components."

The mobile CT inspection system was specifically designed for easy transport and rapid deployment. It offers the same precision and performance as stationary CT installations, but can be installed and put into operation at the production site within a very short time.

## Efficient inspection with customer-specific evaluation

The _SCANEXPRESS_ impresses with its highly efficient way of working: the system enables fast scans and, thanks to the integrated evaluation unit, makes it possible to analyse CT data directly at the container. The evaluation software can be quickly adapted from existing modules to the specific needs of each customer. A particular advantage is the extensibility of the software, which can be enhanced at any time with further component inspections from the customer – a decisive factor for companies with growing or changing inspection requirements.

The internationalisation of the _SCANEXPRESS_ comes at a time when European manufacturers are increasingly looking for efficient quality assurance solutions. Customers from the automotive, aerospace and electronics sectors particularly benefit from being able to integrate CT inspections flexibly into their production processes.

Microvista plans to offer the system initially in the core markets of Germany, France and Italy, before extending availability to further European countries.
