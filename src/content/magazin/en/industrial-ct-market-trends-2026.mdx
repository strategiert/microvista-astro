Industrial computed tomography is evolving rapidly. Here are the most important trends for 2026:

## 1. AI-driven evaluation

Artificial intelligence is revolutionising CT evaluation. Automatic defect detection now reaches recognition rates of more than 99%.

## 2. Mobile CT systems

With systems like SCANEXPRESS we bring inspection directly to the customer. No long transport routes, no waiting times.

## 3. Integration into Industry 4.0

CT systems are increasingly being integrated into networked production environments. Real-time data enables proactive quality assurance.

## Conclusion

Industrial CT faces exciting developments. Companies that adopt these technologies early secure competitive advantages.
