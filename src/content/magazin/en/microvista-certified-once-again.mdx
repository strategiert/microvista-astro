## Audit according to DIN EN ISO 9001:2015

As a company with high quality standards, it is particularly important to implement a quality management system to define standards and thus ensure smooth processes.

In order to check its own quality standards, Microvista has decided to be certified according to **DIN EN ISO 9001:2015**. An independent audit was successfully conducted once again.

![](/images/wp/2023/02/ISO_9001_colour_en1.jpg)

## Significance for Microvista

It is particularly important to Microvista, as a testing and inspection service provider in the field of computed tomography, to test and certify its own standards on a recurring basis. This way, customers can be sure that existing quality management systems are tested and continuously improved.
